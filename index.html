What is Artificer

ArtisanOga is an online recruitment platform for skilled workers in Nigeria. ArtisanOga welcomes all skilled workers, from tailors to plumbers and IT professionals. We help skilled workers monetize their skills by facilitating access to decent jobs that improve their livelihood. We aim to connect 25 million skilled workers in Africa, particularly women and young people, to decent jobs by 2030.
