About Us

Introduction

We are establishing an online artisans platform that seamlessly connects skilled artisans with local markets. Our platform aims to facilitate smooth interactions between consumers and artisans by providing them with an online store to showcase and promote their diverse range of goods and services. This platform will effectively highlight these offerings to both new and loyal customers, fostering a thriving marketplace for artisans and enhancing their visibility in the market.

Artisans

  • Beauticians: Makeup Artist, Nail Tech, Gele Tying.
  • Automobile: Mechanic, Vulcanizer.
  • Cleaning: Dry Cleaner, Car Wash.
  • Home Service: Personal Chef, Vendor, Cleaners, Nanny.
  • Fashion: Stylist.

Basic Features

1. Geo locator: The app operates by utilizing a map that enables seamless functionality, searching for artisans in close proximity to the user. This feature proves particularly valuable in emergency situations, ensuring the app identifies and connects the user with the nearest available artisan. This strategic use of location data nhances the app's efficiency and responsiveness, addressing urgent needs by promptly connecting users with nearby skilled professionals.

Vision

We are developing an online marketplace dedicated to showcasing the unique creations of artisans, empowering them to broaden their customer reach and connect with diverse communities.
